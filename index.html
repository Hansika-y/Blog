Places you Must Visit

Dal Lake, Kashmir

I had visited in July 2022

Dal lake is located in the capital city of Kashmir, Srinagar. It overlooks a beautiful view of the mountains. Dal Lake also has boat houses on which many people stay. Very neatly made, these boathouses act like wonderful hotels to stay in. Often, chefs are hired to cook for you during your stay. On the other side, there are very colorful gardens called the Nishat Bagh.




San Francisco, California

I had visited in January 2023

San Francisco, officially the City and County of San Francisco, is a commercial, financial, and cultural center of Northern California. It has the iconic features such as, Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, buzzing neighborhoods such as Chinatown, and its many museums and historic architecture




Las Vegas, Nevada

I had visited in December 2022

Las Vegas, often known simply as Vegas, is the 25th-most populous city in the United States, the most populous city in the state of Nevada, and the county seat of Clark County. The Las Vegas Valley metropolitan area is the largest within the greater Mojave Desert, and 2nd-largest in the Southwestern United States.




Sedona, Arizona

I had visited in April 2023

Sedona is an Arizona desert town near Flagstaff that’s surrounded by red-rock buttes, steep canyon walls and pine forests. It’s noted for its mild climate and vibrant arts community. Uptown Sedona is dense with New Age shops, spas and art galleries. On the town’s outskirts, numerous trailheads access Red Rock State Park, which offers bird-watching, hiking and picnicking spots.

Review On The Movies

Lion King
Beats Every Other Graphic Out there

I watched Lion King a weed ago with my family and the graphics are amazing. It beats all the other graphics out there. The way that they expressed emotion on all of the animals faces was extraordinary. The whole concept of the movie was very interesting itself.
